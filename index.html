about fitness club


fitness health club provides fitness and empowers those seeking affordable healthcare. Our vision is affordable healthcare and bodycare for all.Our mission is simple — help Needy Peoples to find affordable and convenient healthcare. Every day, we work to gain your trust and provide clear, easy-to-understand information to help you find affordable ways to stay healthy. We achieves its mission by providing information on healthcare programs, offering direct assistance and facilitating programs.fitness club provides a 24/7 Fitness facility to residents of Martinsville and Henry County, as well as surrounding areas to help people reach and maintain their goals. We combine different types of fitness equipment to meet different fitness needs and levels. At fitness club you’ll find all the latest strength and cardio equipment along with an energetic group exercise program that includes POUND, Zumba, Kickboxing, Bootcamp, Muscle Building and many other cardio CLASSES. Our Staff, Trainers & Group Exercise Instructors are committed to offering our members a great fitness experience.
